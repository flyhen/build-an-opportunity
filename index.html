Our Purpose

Moving beyond charity toward lasting opportunity.

Build An Opportunity is a community-centered organization in Uganda focused on addressing systemic poverty. We tackle poverty at its roots by equipping young people and communities with the skills, tools, and dignity they need to build their own future—not just receiving handouts.

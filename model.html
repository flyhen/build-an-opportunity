Our Roots in Mpigi

The serene shores of Lake Victoria basin in Mpigi district are home to a resilient community facing numerous challenges. Despite its breathtaking beauty, life here is marked by struggles for survival.

Families grapple with poverty, limited access to education, and food insecurity. Children often find themselves forced into labor, fishing on the lake instead of pursuing an education. It is within this backdrop of adversity that we are committed to making a difference.
